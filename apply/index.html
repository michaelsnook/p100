    How does it work?

    PCCC staff will review all applications and the most qualified applicants will be contacted for a follow up interview. The 25 finalists will be notified no later than two weeks prior to the training. Apply as soon as possible -- the final deadline is four weeks before the training. After the training, we'll work to place you on a progressive campaign so you can put your skills to work.

    Limited scholarships are available to those in need.
